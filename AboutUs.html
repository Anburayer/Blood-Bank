    • Tamil Nadu's one of the largest and the most Modern Blood Bank is striving to bring a change in Indian Blood Banking. About 24,000 volunteers donate blood to us every year and this is on the rise phenomenally. We serve more than 30,000 patients with about 50,000 blood components per annum "without replacement." We are transforming our activities and reaching out to people across the country.

    Blood Bank has professionals from different arenas with a common objective of "Sharing Life. Saving lives". People who work for Blood Bank have an unmatched capacity for caring and commitment to saving lives.

    Blood Bank's transparent and flexi work culture makes it a great work experience by itself. We believe that the people should enjoy their work to give it their best. Our work culture revolves around the growth of the organization and training them to become future leaders.

    Flat Hierarchy


           Blood banking is not an individual task. It is team work. We believe in human dignity and respect. We promote rather a flat hierarchy where every one works together and helps each others without any bias or boundaries. This helps us to serve the society better.

    Challenging Assignments


          Blood Bank is constantly engaged in education and research activities to promote transfusion medicine in India . This involves blood banking, transfusion transmitted infections and many more such projects. We provide opportunities for youngsters from all over the world to explore new grounds in transfusion medicine and promotion of blood donation.
